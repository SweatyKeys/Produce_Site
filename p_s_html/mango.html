Mango Season

The most popular cultivars of mango have different harvest seasons, which allows for the possibility of mango to be available all year round. Mangos are in high production in Brazil, Ecuador, Guatemala, Mexico and Nicaragua. The most popular of these cultivars are: Haden,Honey, Keitt, Kent, Palmer, and Tommy Atkins.

Ripe Mango

The overall indicator for mango is feel./strong> Some varieties, can indicate a change of color, or even a slight wrinkle of the skin. However, the feel of all ripe mango is the same, which can be the general indicator of what is ready to eat.
