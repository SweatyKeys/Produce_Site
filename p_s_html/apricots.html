Apricot Season

California contributes 90-95% of apricot production in the United States. Harvest begins late May, to mid July . You can find Apricots in August, which is solely from the state of Washington.

Ripe Apricots

Indicators for ripe apricots include color, feel, and size. A ripe apricot has a bright orange color. You will notice some show a bit of red color to their skin, but this is from exposure to more sun, and not an indicator to being more flavorful nor ripe. A ready to eat apricot will be lightly soft, and the more flavorful ones will be smaller in size.
