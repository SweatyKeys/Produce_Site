Pears Season

In general, pear harvest season starts in fall and can end depending on variety, in middle of Winter, all they way till mid Spring. Due to cooling methods to preserve fruit, you can find the most popular varieties all year round, however, the fruit won't be as good as it will be during harvest season. Bartlett pears are harvest in late August, early September through early February. D'anjou pears are available in late September though are most commonly seen available year round. Bosc pears harvest begins in the fall in Oregon and Washington, which lasts till the end of April.
