Papaya Season

Though papayas can be found all year round, their peak season starts early Summer and lasts through fall. A large quantity of papaya are grown in Hawaii, though California, Florida, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Central and South America all contribute to the availability to the large fruits.

Ripe Papaya

There are a few indicators for ripe papaya which arecolor, feel, and smell.Ripe papaya will be predominantly yellow all over. Check the stem site for mold or mildew, and if any present, do not buy or eat. Ripe fruit will yield slightly from gentle pressure to the skin. Smell the papaya from the stem site, where it should have a light sweet scent.
